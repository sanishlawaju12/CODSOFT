Destination

Swayambhunath Temple

Swayambhunath Temple, also known as the Monkey Temple, is a sacred Buddhist site situated atop a hill in Kathmandu, Nepal. It is adorned with a majestic stupa and is surrounded by numerous shrines and monasteries, offering breathtaking views of the city below. With its rich history, vibrant atmosphere, and resident monkey population, Swayambhunath Temple is a must-visit destination for both pilgrims and tourists seeking spiritual enlightenment and cultural immersion in the heart of Nepal.

Ghandruk

Ghandruk is a picturesque village nestled in the Annapurna region of Nepal, renowned for its stunning mountain vistas, traditional Gurung culture, and warm hospitality. Perched on a hillside, this charming settlement offers panoramic views of snow-capped peaks like Annapurna and Machhapuchhre. Visitors can explore narrow stone-paved streets lined with traditional houses, visit ancient monasteries, and experience the vibrant local culture through traditional dances and festivals.

Bhaktapur Durbar Square

Bhaktapur Durbar Square is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in the ancient city of Bhaktapur, Nepal. It is renowned for its exquisite Newari architecture, intricate wood carvings, and well-preserved historical monuments dating back to the Malla dynasty. The square is a vibrant hub of cultural activity, where locals and visitors alike gather to admire the stunning craftsmanship of temples, palaces, and courtyards. From the iconic Nyatapola Temple to the intricately carved Peacock Window.
